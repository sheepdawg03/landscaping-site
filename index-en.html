Take good care of your outdoor and indoor wood to preserve its value.

By keeping the wood beautiful and smooth, you ensure long-lasting durability. This will bring you everyday joy and maximize your home's economic value.

Did you know?
Balcony wood should be cared for twice a year! Once in spring/summer to guard against sun and heat, and again in late summer/autumn to prepare for rain, frost, and snow. Once a year is okay, but many balconies go years without care, and it shows.
